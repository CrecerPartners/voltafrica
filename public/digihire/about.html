About Digihire

Building the sales ecosystem where brands, talent, and opportunity connect

Digihire helps brands grow through structured sales campaigns, recruitment, activations, and training, while helping sales talent access gigs, jobs, and career-building opportunities through one connected platform.

Who We Are

A modern sales network for brands and talent

Digihire is building a modern sales network for brands that need traction and talent that needs opportunity. We exist to connect businesses to real people who can sell — whether that means launching structured campaigns, recruiting sales talent, running field activations, or training the next generation of sales professionals.

We are not just building a platform. We are building the structure that helps brands launch faster, hire smarter, and activate markets more effectively — while helping sales talent earn, grow, and build stronger careers.

Our Vision

To build the most trusted sales ecosystem for brands and talent across Africa — where businesses can access the people they need to grow, and talent can access the opportunities they need to earn, learn, and thrive.

Our Mission

To help brands drive growth through people-powered sales execution while helping sales talent unlock income, experience, and long-term career opportunities. We do this by creating structured systems for campaigns, recruitment, field activations, and sales training — all designed to turn sales into a stronger engine for growth.

What We Do

Supporting both sides of the market

Digihire supports brands that need growth and talent that can sell.

  • Launch structured sales campaigns through VoltSquad
  • Recruit sales talent for full-time, contract, and project-based roles
  • Run field activations and on-ground marketing campaigns

For Talent

Earn, grow, and build a real sales career

  • Join sales campaigns and earn commissions
  • Enter the talent pool for full-time and part-time roles
  • Learn through the Sales Academy and grow into stronger opportunities

Why We Built Digihire

Closing the gap between brands and sales talent

Too many brands struggle to find reliable sales execution. At the same time, too many talented people are looking for real opportunities to earn, work with brands, and grow in sales. Digihire was built to close that gap.

We saw the need for a system that goes beyond ads, beyond random referrals, and beyond scattered hiring. We are building a more structured way for brands to launch campaigns, recruit talent, activate markets, and build traction through real people.

Our Ecosystem

Everything you need in one place

Five connected solutions that power brands and talent on both sides of the market.

VoltSquad

Our campaign marketplace where brands launch structured sales campaigns and sellers earn through real sales activity.

Talent Pool

A structured profile and matching system that helps brands discover sales talent and helps talent access better job opportunities.

Sales Academy

A modern training platform where sales talent can learn, build practical skills, earn certifications, and prepare for stronger opportunities.

Sales Recruitment

End-to-end recruitment support for brands looking for sales reps, closers, field teams, merchandisers, and business development talent.

Activations & Field Marketing

On-ground campaign support that helps brands build visibility, drive engagement, and activate markets across key locations.
